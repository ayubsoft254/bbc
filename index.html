About Busia Beyond Crime

A registered nonprofit organization dedicated to preventing crime, empowering youth, and fostering peaceful communities in Busia County, Kenya.

Our Mission

To create safer, more peaceful communities by empowering youth through sports, preventing crime and extremism, and providing support for the reintegration of ex-convicts into society.

We believe that football and community engagement are powerful tools for positive change, helping young people develop life skills, build confidence, and choose constructive paths forward.

Vision

Peaceful communities free from crime

Values

Integrity, Unity, Empowerment

Message from Our Founder

"Having served in the military, I understand the importance of discipline, community, and second chances. Busia Beyond Crime was born from my conviction that every young person deserves an opportunity to succeed, and every community member deserves to feel safe."

Retired Major Titus Wabwire

Founder & Director, Busia Beyond Crime
